Orange Chicken

Description

This 4-ingredient orange chicken made with frozen chicken tenders is great for when youre craving takeout. Its so simple to make at home, is very budget-friendly, and only takes 4 ingredients. I serve it over rice, topped with green onions and sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orange marmalade
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 pound cooked chicken pieces
  • green onions (optional)
  • sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Place frozen nuggets in a single layer on a baking sheet.

  2. Step 2

    3. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and crispy, 11 to 13 minutes, or according to package directions.

  3. Step 3

    4. Meanwhile, whisk marmalade, BBQ sauce, and soy sauce together in a small saucepan and heat over low heat until hot, about 5 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place nuggets in a large bowl. Drizzle sauce over the top. Toss to coat.
